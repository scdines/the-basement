The Basement is an unexpected experience of the senses located beneath the historic 1933 U.S. Post Office. This underground venue is a home away from home for those seeking the trendiest, tastiest, and most cutting edge concepts of a modern day floormarketplace.

Our mission is to create a multi-use incubator space of both retail and food vendors who all celebrate the craft of the hand. Creating an alternative collaborative culture in the downtown corridor of Reno, NV.
